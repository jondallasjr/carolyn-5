Tourist Visa Extension (Optional 30-Day)

Extend your initial 60-day visa exemption to 90 days total, providing extra time before DTV application.

Timeline

October 19-20, 2025

Apply for 30-Day Tourist Extension

Overview

This optional extension provides an additional 30 days in Thailand, bringing your total tourist stay from 60 days to 90 days. This gives you extra time to:

  • Complete medical consultations and build relationships with Thai healthcare providers
  • Finalize DTV application documents without rushing
  • Ensure all medical records and letters are properly prepared
  • Coordinate with your financial sponsor on required documentation

How to Apply

Step 1: Visit Immigration Office

Go to the Chiang Mai Immigration Office during business hours. The office is located in central Chiang Mai.

Step 2: Prepare Documents

  • Passport (original)
  • TM.7 form (available at immigration office)
  • One passport-sized photo (4x6 cm)
  • Copy of passport information page

Step 3: Pay Fee

Cost: 1,900 THB (approximately $55-60 USD)

Payment made directly at the immigration office.

Step 4: Processing

Processing Time: Same day

Extensions are typically processed the same day you apply. You'll leave with your extended tourist stamp.

Key Details

New Expiration Date

Your tourist visa will be extended by exactly 30 days from your current expiration date. If you arrived August 25 on the 60-day exemption (expiring October 24), your extension brings it to approximately November 23.

Total Stay Duration

90 days total (60-day initial exemption + 30-day extension)

Why This Matters for DTV

The extra 30 days ensures you have sufficient time to complete your DTV application process, including the trip to Vietnam for submission and approval (typically 2-5 working days, but can take up to 2 weeks).

Important Notes

  • Optional: This extension is not required, but highly recommended to avoid rushing the DTV process
  • Single Entry Only: This extension only applies to your current stay. You cannot leave and re-enter Thailand during this extension period
  • Timing: Apply for the extension near the end of your 60-day period, typically 2-3 weeks before expiration
  • No Return Visits: If you travel to Vietnam for DTV application during this extension, you cannot return to Thailand. This is why DTV is applied for while on tourist visa status
  • Future Entry: After receiving your 5-year DTV, you won't need tourist extensions anymore

Next Steps

  1. Complete Medical Planning - Ensure your medical consultation and plan are finalized (see Medical Planning section)
  2. Prepare DTV Documents - Begin compiling all required documents for DTV application
  3. Coordinate with Sponsor - Work with your financial sponsor on their required documentation
  4. Plan Vietnam Trip - Once extension is approved, plan your Vietnam trip for DTV submission (see Vietnam Trip section)
