DTV Application Document Compilation

Complete guide to gathering and organizing all required documents for a successful Destiny Visa (DTV) application. All documents must be error-free—typos cause automatic rejection.

Medical Documents Package

The foundation of your DTV application. These must demonstrate 6+ months of ongoing medical treatment need.

  • Medical consultation letter from Thai doctor (Bangkok Hospital recommended)
  • Detailed medical treatment plan (6+ months minimum)
  • Proof of diagnosis (medical records from US)
  • Physical therapy or treatment schedule
  • Doctor's recommendation for Thailand-based treatment
  • Clinic contact information and stamp/seal
Pro Tip: Bangkok Hospital Chiang Mai has proven track record for DTV medical letters. Schedule in-person consultation ($40-90) to build relationship with doctor.

Financial Sponsor Documents

Your financial sponsor commits to covering emergency repatriation costs if needed.

  • Sponsor bank statements (3+ months showing 500,000+ THB / $15,000+ USD)
  • Sponsorship letter on official letterhead:
    • Commitment to financial support
    • Specific mention of emergency repatriation costs
    • Relationship to applicant
    • Sponsor signature and date
  • Proof of relationship (birth certificate or equivalent)
  • Sponsor passport copy
Critical: Bank statements must clearly show the 500,000 THB balance maintained for entire 3+ month period. Recent deposits don't count—funds must be stable.

Personal Identity Documents

  • Valid US passport (copy and original)
  • Passport bio page (clear color copy)
  • Passport signature page (copy)
  • Birth certificate (certified copy)
  • Emergency contact list:
    • US primary care doctor
    • US hospital
    • Financial sponsor (Jon)
    • US family members

Application Package Assembly

Prepare documents in the following order:

  1. Completed DTV online application form
  2. Passport bio page
  3. Medical consultation letter
  4. Medical treatment plan
  5. US medical records (surgeries, MRI, X-rays)
  6. Sponsor bank statements (3+ pages showing 500,000+ THB)
  7. Sponsorship letter
  8. Birth certificate
  9. Proof of relationship to sponsor
  10. Emergency contact list
Format: All documents should be scanned/photographed clearly, organized in PDF files with clear naming conventions.

Critical Quality Checklist

Before submitting, verify:

  • ✓ No spelling errors in any document
  • ✓ Consistent name spelling across all documents
  • ✓ All dates are clear and legible
  • ✓ Signatures and stamps are visible
  • ✓ Medical letterhead is clear with clinic contact info
  • ✓ Bank statements show full name and account details
  • ✓ All required elements included (no missing pages)
  • ✓ Documents are in chronological order
  • ✓ File sizes are reasonable for upload
  • ✓ Colors are accurate (not over-darkened or faded)

Application Submission

Submit online at thaievisa.go.th

  • Submit within 24 hours of arrival in Vietnam
  • Non-refundable application fee: $340
  • Online-only applications (as of October 2024)
  • Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City have very high approval rates
Processing Timeline:
  • Official: 5 working days
  • Realistic: 2 days to 2 weeks
  • Cannot return to Thailand during processing—must stay in Vietnam
  • Approval arrives via email; print multiple copies immediately

After DTV Approval

  • Print multiple copies of DTV approval email
  • Fly back to Chiang Mai with printed approval
  • Present DTV approval at Thailand border
  • Entry stamp valid for 5 years with 180-day stays
  • No re-entry permits needed for entire 5-year period
