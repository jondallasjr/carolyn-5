Vietnam Trip for DTV Application

Travel to Vietnam to submit your DTV visa application at the Thai embassy in Hanoi or Ho Chi Minh City.

Trip Timeline

Nov 10-12, 2025

Travel to Vietnam

Notes:
  • Travel with your Financial Sponsor to finalize and submit DTV application
  • Budget: ~$50-100 for domestic flight
  • Bring all required documents in organized folder
Nov 12-19, 2025

Stay in Vietnam During Processing

⚠️ CRITICAL:
  • Cannot Return to Thailand during processing—must stay in Vietnam
  • Embassy may request additional documents via email
  • Check email regularly for approval notification
  • Budget: $400-600 total for Vietnam stay (visa + accommodation + food)
Nov 19, 2025

Return to Thailand with DTV

✓ Success:
  • No re-entry permits needed for 5 years
  • This solves visa issues for 5+ years
  • Can stay continuously for up to 360 days with one extension (~$300)
  • Or do border run every 180 days for fresh entry

Embassy Information

Thai Embassy - Hanoi

High approval rate for DTV applications from US citizens

  • Processing: 2-5 working days typical
  • Located: Central Hanoi
  • City benefits: Budget accommodation, good food scene

Thai Embassy - Ho Chi Minh City

Also high approval rate for DTV applications

  • Processing: 2-5 working days typical
  • Located: Central Ho Chi Minh City
  • City benefits: More expats, more Western amenities

Key Reminders

  • All documents must be error-free—typos cause automatic rejection
  • Bring originals AND copies of all required documents
  • Financial Sponsor attendance is typically required for DTV application submission
  • Check email daily during processing for any requests from embassy
  • Have 500,000+ THB in sponsor's account for 3+ months (verified)
  • Print DTV approval in color and bring multiple copies to border
  • Vietnam visa can be obtained online before travel or on arrival (e-visa available)
