Medical Planning & DTV Foundation

Setting up your medical consultation, establishing a treatment plan, and creating the documentation foundation required for DTV visa approval.

Get a Medical Plan (required for DTV)

September 16 - October 14, 2025

Key Tasks

  • Contact Bangkok Hospital in Chiang Mai
  • Schedule in-person consultation (~$40-90)
  • Review medical records with Thai doctor
  • Get a 6 month+ medical plan for ongoing PT/pain management
Important Notes:
  • This consultation creates the foundation for visa approval
  • Bangkok Hospital has proven track record for DTV medical letters
  • Must show 6+ months treatment needed
  • Physical therapy costs $25-75/session vs $100+ in US

Medical Documentation Requirements

Your medical plan forms the cornerstone of your DTV application. Here's what you need:

  • Medical Letter: From Bangkok Hospital or similar English-speaking facility outlining your condition and need for continued treatment
  • Treatment Plan: Detailed plan showing 6+ months of ongoing medical care (physical therapy, pain management, etc.)
  • Cost Documentation: Evidence of why treatment in Thailand is necessary and cost-effective
  • US Medical Records: Previous surgeries, MRI results, X-rays, and medical history to support your case

Bangkok Hospital Consultation Tips

  • Request an appointment with a doctor experienced in DTV medical letters
  • Bring all US medical records and documentation
  • Discuss your specific treatment needs and timeline
  • Ask about ongoing physical therapy availability and costs
  • Request the doctor write a detailed medical letter specifically for visa purposes
  • Get multiple copies of all medical documentation

Cost: This consultation is an investment (~$40-90) that directly enables your DTV approval and 5-year stay.

Why This Matters for Your DTV

The DTV (Destination Thailand Visa) requires proof of a legitimate reason to stay in Thailand for an extended period. Your medical treatment plan demonstrates:

  • Clear, documented need for ongoing medical care
  • Legitimate reason to stay for 5+ years
  • Connection to a reputable Thai medical facility
  • Financial understanding of treatment costs

Next Step: After securing your medical plan and documentation, you'll use this foundation to prepare your complete DTV application package.
