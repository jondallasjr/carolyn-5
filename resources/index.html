Resources & Links Directory

Essential links, government websites, medical facilities, expat communities, and practical resources for Thailand relocation and the DTV visa process.

Government & Visa Resources

  • Thai eVisa Portal (thaievisa.go.th) - Official DTV and tourist visa applications
  • Thai Ministry of Foreign Affairs - Official government information
  • Royal Thai Embassy - Washington DC - US consular services
  • Royal Thai Embassy (Global Directory) - Find embassies worldwide
  • Thai Immigration Bureau - Official immigration information
  • US Embassy Bangkok - US citizen services in Thailand

Medical & Healthcare Facilities

  • Bangkok Hospital Chiang Mai - Recommended for DTV medical consultations
  • Bumrungrad International Hospital - Premium healthcare, Bangkok
  • Health Care Medical Clinic - Dr. Morgan (recommended primary care, Chiang Mai) - Contact through Bangkok Hospital
  • Samitivej Hospital - International hospitals in Thailand
  • International SOS - Emergency medical services

Expat Communities & Support Groups

  • Chiang Mai Expats Facebook Group - Active community (search on Facebook)
  • Chiang Mai AA Meetings - 10+ meetings per week, various languages
  • Refuge Recovery / Buddhist Recovery - Recovery communities in Chiang Mai
  • The Train Studio - Art therapy and creative communities
  • Counselling Thailand - Therapy and counseling services
  • The Cabin Alumni Network - Addiction recovery support

Practical Services & Transportation

  • Grab App - Taxis, motorbikes, and ride-sharing in Thailand
  • Airbnb - Short-term accommodation in Chiang Mai
  • Booking.com - Hotels and guesthouses
  • AIS, Dtac, True Move - Thai mobile phone providers (get SIM at airport)
  • XE.com - Currency exchange rates
  • Revolut or Wise - Low-fee international money transfers

Housing & Suthep Area Resources

  • Facebook Marketplace - Housing listings for Chiang Mai
  • DDproperty.com - Thai property portal
  • Suthep/Nimman Areas - Large expat community with English-speaking services, restaurants, and amenities
  • Typical Costs (Suthep area) - Housing $250-500/month, cleaning service 500-800 THB ($15-24) for 2-3 hours

Financial & Banking

  • Thai ATM Withdrawal - Typically 220 THB ($6.50) fee per transaction
  • Siam Commercial Bank (SCB) - One of Thailand's largest banks
  • Kasikornbank (K Bank) - Major Thai bank
  • Bangkok Bank - Thailand's largest bank
  • DTV Financial Requirements: Financial sponsor needs 500,000+ THB ($15,000+ USD) for 3+ months

Vietnam Travel Resources (for DTV Application)

  • Thai Embassy in Hanoi & Ho Chi Minh City - High DTV approval rates
  • Vietnam E-Visa - US citizens need visa ($25 online) or visa-on-arrival
  • Accommodation Budget - Vietnam: $30-40/night, total trip budget $400-600
  • Booking.com or Agoda - Vietnam hotel reservations
  • Vietnam E-Visa Portal - Official online visa application

Travel Health Insurance

  • SafetyWing - Affordable travel/expat health insurance
  • World Nomads - Travel insurance for digital nomads
  • Allianz Global - International health insurance
  • Expatica - Expat insurance resources

Research & Planning Resources

  • Numbeo - Cost of living comparisons
  • XE Currency Charts - Historical exchange rates
  • Time and Date - Time zones and conversion
  • Weather Underground - Thailand climate and air quality
  • Burning Season Info - March-May in Northern Thailand; consider southern Thailand or Bali during worst air quality

Important Notes

  • Document Quality: All DTV documents must be error-free; typos cause automatic rejection
  • Visa Exemption: US citizens receive automatic 60-day visa exemption; no pre-approval needed
  • Medical Records: Bring US medical records, surgeries, MRI, X-rays for DTV medical consultation
  • DTV Processing: Official timeline is 5 working days from Vietnam; actual times vary 2 days to 2 weeks
  • Cannot Return During DTV Processing: Must stay in Vietnam until approval received via email
  • Medication Availability: Many US medications available in Thailand at fraction of US cost
