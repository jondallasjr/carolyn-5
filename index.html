Key Information

What is the DTV Visa?

Thailand's Destination Thailand Visa (DTV) is a long-term visa for digital nomads, remote workers, and those seeking treatment abroad. It's valid for 5 years and allows stays of up to 180-360 days.

Why Chiang Mai?

Large expat community, affordable medical care (physical therapy $25-75/session vs $100+ in US), extensive AA meetings and recovery support, low cost of living ($250-500/month housing), and beautiful mountain setting.

Initial Entry

US citizens get automatic 60-day visa exemption on arrival. No advance visa required. Apply for 30-day extension if needed to reach 90 days total before DTV application.

Medical Plan Requirement

DTV approval requires documented medical treatment plan from Thai hospital. Consultation costs $40-90. Must show 6+ months of ongoing treatment (physical therapy, counseling, etc.).

Financial Requirements

Financial sponsor must show $15,000+ USD ($500,000 THB) in bank account for 3+ months and commit to emergency support.

Application Process

Submit application at Thai embassy in Vietnam within 24 hours of arrival. Official processing: 5 working days. Realistic: 2 days to 2 weeks. Cannot return to Thailand during processing.
